# PROJECT SCOPE AUDIT: THE DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION
**DIRTY DOGS MTL | OFFICIAL TECHNICAL DOSSIER | APRIL 2026**

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This audit documents the end-to-end digital orchestration of the Dirty Dogs MTL platform. The project transition from a legacy presence to a high-fidelity, artisan-engineered ecosystem.

---

## I. BRAND ARCHITECTURE & VISUAL DNA
- **Strategic Foundation**: Brand Story, Mission, Vision, and "Sophisticated Rebel" Personality.
- **Visual Identity**: Tiered Logo Architecture, Chromatic Standards, and Physical Touchpoint Design.

## II. PRODUCT DESIGN & FRONTEND ORCHESTRATION
- **High-Fidelity Interface**: Responsive React 19 / Vite 7 platform with 38px Squircle geometry.
- **UI/UX Excellence**: Glassmorphism, conversion-optimized menu flows, and accessibility engines.
- **Partner Integration**: Design and development of branded UI hubs for Uber Eats and DoorDash.

## III. BACKEND INFRASTRUCTURE & DATA ARCHITECTURE
- **Firebase Ecosystem**: Deployment of serverless App Hosting environment with SSL/Security hardening.
- **Data Orchestration**: Design of the artisan JSON menu schema for autonomous management.
- **Edge Performance**: Configuration of a Global Edge CDN and CI/CD pipelines.

## IV. SEO & PERFORMANCE OPTIMIZATION
- **Semantic Shadow**: A 10,000+ character metadata layer for organic crawler discoverability.
- **PWA Infrastructure**: Implementation of an offline-ready Progressive Web App ecosystem.
