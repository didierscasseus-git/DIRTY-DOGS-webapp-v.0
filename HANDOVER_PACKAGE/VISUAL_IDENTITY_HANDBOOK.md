# VISUAL IDENTITY HANDBOOK: BRAND GUIDELINES
**DIRTY DOGS MTL | OFFICIAL DESIGN STANDARDS | APRIL 2026**

## 1. BRAND STORY AND MISSION
### A. Our Story
Dirty Dogs started on Saint-Laurent Blvd to offer real, handmade street food. We focus on slow-cooked, high-quality meals for people who want authentic Montreal flavor.

### B. Our Mission
To provide great street food that combines a casual urban feel with quality cooking.

### C. Our Vision
To be the top choice for street food in Montreal, known for our unique style and consistent quality.

---

## 2. BRAND PERSONALITY
- **Personality**: Bold, Energetic, Straightforward, Iconic.
- **Tone of Voice**: Friendly but confident. We are proud of our food and our location.
- **Main Tagline**: "Original Street Food."
- **Second Tagline**: "The Corner of Saint-Laurent."

---

## 3. LOGO AND VISUALS
### A. The Lexicon Logo
- **Description**: The official text-based logo of Dirty Dogs MTL.
- **Usage**: This is our primary identifier. Use it on the website header and all official communications.

### B. Graffiti Background
- **Description**: The urban graffiti artwork used as a background style.
- **Role**: This represents our brand's "street" energy but is not the logo itself. Use it to add texture and personality to digital and physical spaces.

### C. Stacked Logo
- **Description**: The version with the "Mustard Dog" icon.
- **Usage**: Use this for business documents, invoices, and specific menu sections.

---

## 4. FUNCTIONAL ICONS
Our digital interface uses a set of clean, high-contrast icons to help customers navigate the menu.

- **Canteen (Utensils)**: Used for the main food menu and signature comfort food.
- **Salchipapas (Loaded Fries)**: A custom-designed icon representing our signature loaded fries bowl with sausage and toppings.
- **The Bar (Beer/Glass)**: Used for our drink selection, including craft beers and house mocktails.

---

## 5. COLORS AND FONTS
- **Main Black (#000000)**: Used for backgrounds.
- **Bright White (#FFFFFF)**: Used for main text.
- **Mustard Yellow (#FFD700)**: Used for buttons and highlights.
- **Fonts**: 
    - **Headlines**: Bebas Neue (All Caps).
    - **Body Text**: Inter (Simple and easy to read).

---

## 5. DESIGN ELEMENTS
We use rounded corners (38px radius) on all boxes and buttons to give the website a modern, clean look. This helps balance the "street" style with a professional feel.
